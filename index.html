About me

I am a PhD student at the Institute of Molecular Biosciences under the supervision of Joseph Powell. My main research interests consist on understanding the genetic control of gene expression and its role in disease at a single cell resolution.

As part of my PhD, I am developing new computational and statistical methods to predict cell types and states from single cell expression data.

The purpose of this project is to create and compile artworks in order to increase the value of genomics research through non-conventional means
